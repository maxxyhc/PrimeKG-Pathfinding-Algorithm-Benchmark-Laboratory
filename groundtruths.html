Ground Truth Pathways

We curate mechanistically validated drug–mechanism–disease pathways from literature and pharmacological databases. Each pathway is represented as a sequence of nodes (drug → targets → pathways / processes → disease) in PrimeKG.

Below is an interactive graph visualization of all curated ground truth pathways.

Methodology

  1. Curate Ground Truth Pathways: Collect validated drug–mechanism–disease pathways from literature and pharmacological databases.
  2. Implement Pathfinding Algorithms: Benchmark BFS, Dijkstra, A*, PageRank‑inverse, and learned‑edge‑weight algorithms on PrimeKG.
  3. Benchmark Against Ground Truth: Evaluate how often algorithms recover the mechanistically correct pathway vs. taking hub‑driven shortcuts.
  4. Develop Quality Metrics: Score paths based on biological plausibility, hub avoidance, and mechanistic coherence, not just shortest distance.
